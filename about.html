Our Story, mission and philosophy

Baker's Point is Toronto's premium online cake store. For us at Baker's Point baking is not just a job, it is our passion. We do our best to ensure that every single product sold from us is of the best quality made with the most important ingredient LOVE. Baker's Point started in 2017 when our founder, an amateur baker who was a college student at that time decided to sell her baked goodies just for the sake of some pocket money. The business flourished over the years and now we have a passionate team of bakers driven by our founder with the same spirit as then.

We specialize in custom orders, providing the customers the freedom to get exactly what they want and how they want it. We provide the experience from cookies to wedding cakes no matter how big or small the order is. We ensure the value for your money for each and every product available. We have provided an open review page for our products which everyone can view and explore. We take our customer opinions very highly and thats the only way we have grown.
